About Our Barbershop

Barbershop Elegance has been serving gentlemen since 2010, combining traditional barbering techniques with modern style. Our shop offers a unique atmosphere where classic craftsmanship meets contemporary comfort.

Step into our elegant space and experience the art of grooming in an environment designed for the modern gentleman who appreciates quality, precision, and timeless style.

Our Story: Founded by 4 enthusiast students from SE-2411, our barbershop was born from a passion for traditional grooming excellence. Over the years, we've built a reputation for attention to detail and customer satisfaction.

Expert Team: Our team consists of certified professionals with years of experience in classic and contemporary styling techniques. We continuously train to stay updated with the latest trends while honoring time-tested traditions.

Our Mission: We believe every man deserves to look and feel his best. That's why we provide personalized consultations and premium services in a relaxing, sophisticated environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a comprehensive range of professional barbering services including classic men's haircuts, traditional straight razor shaves, beard trimming and styling, mustache grooming, and complete grooming packages. All services include professional consultation and styling advice to help you achieve your perfect look.

You can easily book an appointment through multiple convenient methods: our online booking form on the website, by calling us at +7 (700) 123-4567, via WhatsApp, or by visiting us in person at our barbershop. We highly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

Yes, we welcome walk-in customers when time permits, though priority is given to clients with scheduled appointments. To avoid waiting time and ensure availability, we recommend calling ahead or booking online through our convenient booking system.

We're open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:30 PM, Sunday is a day off. Please note that holiday hours may vary. For the most current schedule information, please call us or check our website.

We accept various payment methods for your convenience: cash, all major credit and debit cards, and modern mobile payment options including Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay. Tips can be added to card payments or given in cash directly to your barber.

A classic haircut typically takes 30-45 minutes, including consultation and styling. More complex styles or combinations (like haircut + beard trim) may take 60-75 minutes. Our masters take their time to ensure precision and quality, never rushing through your service.

No, you don't need to bring anything! We provide all necessary tools, products, and supplies. However, if you have specific hair products you prefer or reference photos of styles you like, feel free to bring them. We also recommend arriving 5-10 minutes early for your first visit to complete our client form.

We understand that plans change. Please give us at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. You can call, text, or use our online booking system to make changes. Last-minute cancellations (less than 4 hours before your appointment) may result in a cancellation fee.

Yes! We offer a 10% discount for students (with valid student ID) and seniors (65+). We also have special combo packages and loyalty programs for regular clients. Ask your barber about our current promotions and membership options during your visit.

Absolutely! When booking online or by phone, you can request your preferred master. Each of our six barbers has unique specialties and styles. Check out our Masters page to learn about Alexander, Marcus, James, Brandon, Tyler, and Alex Turner (yes, the former Arctic Monkeys frontman!).

We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene. All tools are sanitized between clients, we use disposable blades for straight razor shaves, capes and towels are laundered after each use, and our stations are thoroughly cleaned throughout the day. Your health and safety are our top priorities.
